Witch Rise

A downloadable game for Windows

Buy Now$4.99 USD or more

Witch Rise tells the story about a light hearted girl who loves learning about magic and was turned into a piglet by the Fallen Witch, a powerful sorcerer who once was beloved and respected but got her heart corrupted by her own greed.

Play as a young girl in the quest for finding a way to get back to her old self again. In your journey you'll need to find the four magic staffs in order to have a chance to defeat the Fallen Witch who bewitched the girl! 

Fight many magical creatures and ancient guardians, while search for very powerful itens which will be really helpful along the way, in this magical themed FPS inspired by the classics and featuring a leveling system, metroidvania style exploration map and 2D sprites in a 3D environment.

FEATURES

- Travel through 4 different biomes;
- Medieval fantasy inspired themes;
- 2D pixelated graphics set on a 3D world;
- A bunch of useful Items to find;
- Simplified leveling system;
- Open World with many alternative paths.
